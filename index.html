Real-Bottle AP/NP Validation Videos

We further evaluate cross-condition transfer on a real wine bottle with a pumpable vacuum stopper. The atmospheric-pressure (AP) condition uses the stopper without pumping and serves as the easier condition, while the negative-pressure (NP) condition pumps the stopper 70 times before each trial, creating higher suction-related removal resistance. A handheld force gauge verifies that NP requires approximately 22 N peak external removal force.
